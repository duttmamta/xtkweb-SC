Xtreck Carbon

Xtreck Carbon is a cloud-based carbon accounting platform that helps SMEs measure, manage and report their emissions with accuracy and ease. It converts operational and financial data into structured, auditable carbon insights, enabling businesses to meet sustainability, supply chain and procurement requirements while identifying opportunities to reduce costs and improve efficiency.
