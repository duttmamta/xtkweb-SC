About Xtreck Group

One group. Two specialist business offerings.

Xtreck Group is structured to deliver focused solutions in sustainability and business support through dedicated service lines.

Our structure

Xtreck Group acts as the umbrella company behind two distinct offerings, each designed around a clear business need.

Xtreck Carbon is the carbon accounting platform offering, built to help organisations organise emissions-related information and strengthen sustainability reporting.

Xtreck Support delivers a complete ownership model of business support, enabling companies to rely on a committed partner for operational continuity and execution.

Core positioning

  • Umbrella brand with separate service identities
  • Clear pathways for sustainability and support services
  • Built for credibility, clarity and growth
  • Ready to expand with future offerings
