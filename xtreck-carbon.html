Xtreck Carbon

Real-time carbon intelligence for SMEs.

Xtreck Carbon is a digital platform that combines real-time energy monitoring with automated carbon reporting to help SMEs generate accurate emissions data.

The problem with traditional carbon reporting

  • Reliance on estimates and utility bill data
  • Limited visibility into operational energy use
  • Reduced confidence for reporting, planning and compliance

Our solution

Xtreck Carbon replaces estimated, bill-based reporting with measured operational insights, enabling better decision-making, cost reduction and compliance with growing sustainability requirements.

Key features

Real-time monitoring

Capture energy usage from operations with stronger visibility than static utility data alone.

Automated reporting

Turn measured data into structured carbon reporting outputs.

Better decisions

Use timely operational insight to identify inefficiencies and prioritise action.

Compliance support

Strengthen reporting readiness as sustainability expectations continue to grow.

Why Xtreck Carbon?

It helps SMEs move from estimated emissions to measured insight — improving confidence, accuracy and actionability.
