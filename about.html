About Xtreck Group

A focused group built around sustainability and business continuity.

Xtreck Group brings together two distinct offerings: Xtreck Carbon, a measured-data carbon platform for SMEs, and Xtreck Support, a complete ownership model for business operations and critical systems.

Our role

As an umbrella company, Xtreck Group exists to create practical, scalable solutions that help organisations operate better and respond to growing market expectations.

We focus on areas where businesses need more than fragmented tools: accurate sustainability insight and dependable long-term operational support.

What sits under Xtreck Group

  • Xtreck Carbon — real-time energy monitoring and automated carbon reporting
  • Xtreck Support — end-to-end ownership of business systems and operations
