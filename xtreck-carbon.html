Why Xtreck Carbon

Many SMEs rely on spreadsheets or estimated data when calculating their carbon footprint. This approach can be time-consuming, inconsistent and difficult to validate.

Xtreck Carbon provides a structured, automated approach to carbon accounting, helping organisations improve accuracy, reduce manual effort and generate auditable emissions data.

It replaces estimated, bill-based reporting with measured operational insights, enabling better decision-making, cost reduction and compliance with growing sustainability requirements.

What the Platform Does

Xtreck Carbon enables businesses to:

  • Capture and organise energy, operational and financial data
  • Convert activity data into emissions using recognised standards such as DEFRA
  • Generate structured carbon reports aligned with industry frameworks
  • Track emissions over time and identify improvement opportunities
