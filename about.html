Xtreck Group

Xtreck Group is an umbrella company bringing together two focused offerings: Xtreck Carbon and Xtreck Support.

Our Focus

We build and support digital solutions that help organisations operate more effectively while responding to growing sustainability and commercial pressures.

Our approach combines practical technology, structured data and long-term support to help businesses improve confidence in both operational delivery and future planning.

Our Offerings

Xtreck Carbon

A carbon accounting platform that combines real-time energy monitoring with automated reporting to help SMEs generate more accurate emissions data.

Xtreck Support

A complete ownership model for business operations and systems, providing continuity, stability and dependable long-term support.

  • Practical technology with real business value
  • Long-term partnerships and dependable delivery
